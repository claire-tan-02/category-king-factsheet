Q 我可以将票转让给他人吗？ Can I transfer my ticket to someone else?
A 可以。票务不可退款，但可以在同一公司内部转让。请在课程开始前 5 个工作日通知我们，以便更新出席者信息。 Yes. Tickets are non-refundable but transferable within the same company. Just inform us 5 working days before the workshop so we can update your attendee information.
Q 有提供翻译服务吗？ Is there any translation service?
A 本课程全程以中文授课，不提供翻译服务。 The workshop will be conducted entirely in Chinese, and no translation will be provided.
Q 本课程可申请 HRDC 补贴吗？ Is the workshop HRDC Claimable?
A 可以。《行业唯王》课程可通过 HRD Corp 培训计划进行补贴申请，具体以贵公司的资格及征收余额为准。 Yes. Category King is HRD Corp claimable under the HRD Corp training schemes, subject to your company's eligibility and levy balance.
